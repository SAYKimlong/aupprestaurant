Baycha Lok Lak

Baycha Lok Lak is a traditional Khmer dish consisting of tender marinated beef stir-fried with aromatic herbs and spices, served alongside fresh vegetables and a zesty dipping sauce, offering a delightful blend of flavors and textures.

Fruit Salad

Fruit salad is special because it's a healthy, customizable mix of colorful fruits, perfect for any occasion.

Yahorn Soup

Savor our special Yahorn Soup, an Asian delight with flavorful broth, tender meat, and aromatic spices that will warm your heart with each delicious sip.

Christmas Special

A Christmas special dish brings together cherished flavors and traditions, symbolizing holiday warmth and celebration. It's a centerpiece of festive gatherings, embodying the spirit of Christmas joy with its rich tastes and nostalgic aromas.

Berries for Breakfast

Berries for breakfast provide a flavorful and nutritious start to the day. Rich in vitamins, antioxidants, and fiber, they support heart health, aid digestion, and boost immunity. Enjoy them with cereal, in smoothies, or with yogurt for a delicious and wholesome morning meal.
